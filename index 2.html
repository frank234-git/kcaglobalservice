About Us

KCA Global Services Nigeria is a multi-dimensional enterprise rooted in excellence and driven by impact. Headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria, we operate across several sectors—procurement, logistics, insurance advisory, and academic support services—delivering dependable solutions to clients locally and internationally. Founded on principles of integrity, reliability, and innovation, KCA Global Services is more than a service provider—we are your trusted growth partner. Through specialized units like KCA Logistics and KCA Library, we streamline the delivery of essential goods and knowledge-based services. Whether it’s sourcing and shipping critical materials, protecting lives through insurance, or empowering students and researchers with expert academic support, we are here to help you thrive. Our team is made up of skilled professionals who are passionate about client satisfaction, efficiency, and results. At KCA, we tailor our services to meet your exact needs—no two clients are the same, and that’s exactly how we approach every engagement. At KCA Global Services, we don’t just promise quality—we deliver it, every single time.
